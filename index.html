Crystal Collector

Game Play

You will be give a random number at the start of the game. The object of the game is to match your total score with the random number.
Each of the four crystals below have a specific hidden value. By clicking on a crystal that value/number of points will be added to your total score. Each time the game restarts, the hidden values of the crystals will change.
If you match your score with the random number you win, if your total score exceeds the random number, you lose.
